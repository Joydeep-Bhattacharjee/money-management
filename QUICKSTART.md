# Quick Start Guide - Money Manager

## Getting Started in 5 Minutes

### Step 1: Start the Application
```bash
npm run dev
```
The app will be available at **http://localhost:3000**

### Step 2: Create Your Account
1. Click **"Create Account"** on the home page
2. Enter:
   - Name: Your full name
   - Email: A valid email address
   - Password: At least 8 characters
3. Click **"Sign Up"**

### Step 3: Create Your First Category
1. Click **"Categories"** in the navbar
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Category Name**: e.g., "Groceries", "Salary", "Utilities"
   - **Color**: Pick a color to identify it
   - **Type**: Choose "Expense" or "Income"
3. Click **"Add Category"**

### Step 4: Add Your First Transaction
1. Click **"Transactions"** in the navbar
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Amount**: Enter the amount
   - **Type**: Choose "Expense" or "Income"
   - **Date**: When did this happen?
   - **Category**: Select from your categories
   - **Description**: (Optional) Add details like "Groceries from Walmart"
3. Click **"Add Transaction"**

### Step 5: View Your Dashboard
1. Click **"Dashboard"** in the navbar
2. See your financial summary:
   - Total Income this month
   - Total Expenses this month
   - Your Balance
   - Number of transactions

## Troubleshooting

**"User already exists"**
- This email is already registered. Sign in instead or use a different email.

**Transactions not showing**
- Make sure you're looking at the correct month
- Check that transactions have the correct date

**Can't add category**
- Category name might already exist
- Try a different name
